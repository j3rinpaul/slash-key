About Slash Key

Slash Key is a 30-hour Hackathon organized by the IEEE Computer Society Kerala Chapter and hosted by the IEEE Computer Society Student Branch Chapter at Cochin University of Science and Technology, Ernakulam in May 2023. During hackathons, participants work together in teams to develop solutions to a specific problem or challenge.

About IEEE CS KS

The IEEE Computer Society Kerala Chapter is a professional organization for computer science and engineering professionals in the Kerala. It aims to promote the advancement of computer science and engineering in region by providing oppurtunities for education, networking and professional development. The society organizes a variety of events, including the technical talks, workshops, seminars, and conferences, to facilitate the exchange of knowledge and ideas among professionals in the field. The society also provides support for students pursuing computer science and engineering education through various initatives, such as student chapters, student competitions and scholarships. Thus, the IEEE Computer Society Kerala Chapter plays a viral role in advancing computer science and engineering in Kerala.

About IEEE CUSAT SB

Pre Events

Idea Pitching Competition

Participants will have the opportunity to present their ideas in a short pitch format, highlighting the problem they are solving, and the potential impact of their project.

Pre-Hackathon

A mini version of the main Hackathon event, typically lasting around 10 hours. It provides participants with an opportunity to experience the process of developing a solution to a specific problem within a shorter time frame. Participants work in teams to ideate, prototype, and present their solutions, with the event serving as a practice run for the main Hackathon. The pre-hackathon allows participants to get a feel for the format and build their skills, ensuring they are well-prepared for the main event.
